# Settings

Customize your IDICO IPMS experience with personal preferences.

## Accessing Settings

1. Click your profile avatar in the header
2. Select **Settings** from the menu
3. Or navigate directly to the Settings page

## Appearance

### Theme

Choose your preferred color scheme:

| Theme | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Light** | Bright background, dark text |
| **Dark** | Dark background, light text |
| **System** | Follows your device preference |

To change theme:
1. Go to **Settings > Appearance**
2. Select your preferred theme
3. Changes apply immediately

### Compact Mode

Reduce spacing for more content on screen:
- Toggle in **Settings > Appearance > Compact Mode**

## Language

### Changing Language

1. Go to **Settings > Language**
2. Select from available languages:
   - English
   - Tiếng Việt (Vietnamese)
3. Click **Save**

The interface updates immediately.

### Regional Settings

Configure date, time, and number formats:
- **Date Format**: DD/MM/YYYY or MM/DD/YYYY
- **Time Format**: 12-hour or 24-hour
- **First Day of Week**: Sunday or Monday

## Notifications

See [Notifications Guide](/docs/user/features/notifications) for detailed settings.

Quick settings:
- **Push Notifications** - Enable/disable
- **Email Notifications** - Enable/disable
- **Sound** - On/off

## Security

### Password

Change your password:
1. Go to **Settings > Security > Password**
2. Enter current password
3. Enter new password
4. Confirm and save

### Active Sessions

View and manage devices where you're logged in:
1. Go to **Settings > Security > Sessions**
2. Review active sessions
3. Sign out of any unrecognized devices

### Login History

View recent login activity:
- Date and time
- Device/browser
- Location (approximate)

## Privacy

### Data & Privacy

Control your data:
- **Download Data** - Export your information
- **Delete Account** - Permanently remove your account

### Profile Visibility

Control who can see your profile:
- Public
- Organization only
- Private

## Accessibility

### Text Size

Adjust text size for readability:
- Small
- Medium (default)
- Large
- Extra Large

### Reduced Motion

Disable animations for better focus:
- Toggle in **Settings > Accessibility > Reduced Motion**

### High Contrast

Increase color contrast:
- Toggle in **Settings > Accessibility > High Contrast**

## Data & Storage

### Clear Cache

Remove cached data to free up space:
1. Go to **Settings > Data & Storage**
2. Click **Clear Cache**
3. Confirm

### Offline Data

Manage offline-available content:
- View cached pages
- Clear offline data
- Configure auto-sync

## About

### App Information

View details about the application:
- Version number
- Build date
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy

### Support

Get help:
- Contact support
- View documentation
- Report an issue

## Resetting to Defaults

To reset all settings to default:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to bottom
3. Click **Reset to Defaults**
4. Confirm your choice

This will not affect your account data.
