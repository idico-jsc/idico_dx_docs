# Profile Management

Learn how to view and update your personal profile information.

## Accessing Your Profile

1. Click on your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown menu
3. Or navigate to **Settings > Profile**

## Profile Information

### Personal Details

Your profile includes:

- **Full Name** - Your display name
- **Email Address** - Your login email (may not be editable)
- **Phone Number** - Contact number
- **Profile Picture** - Your avatar image

### Updating Information

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Click **Edit Profile** or the edit icon
3. Modify the fields you want to update
4. Click **Save Changes**

## Profile Picture

### Uploading a New Picture

1. Click on your current avatar
2. Select **Change Photo** or click the camera icon
3. Choose an image from your device
4. Crop the image if needed
5. Click **Save**

### Image Requirements

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or GIF
- **Size**: Maximum 5MB
- **Dimensions**: At least 200x200 pixels
- **Shape**: Will be cropped to a circle

### Removing Picture

1. Click on your avatar
2. Select **Remove Photo**
3. Confirm the removal

Your avatar will revert to your initials.

## Changing Your Password

### From Profile Settings

1. Go to **Settings > Security** or **Profile > Change Password**
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter your new password
4. Confirm your new password
5. Click **Update Password**

### Password Requirements

Your new password must:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include at least one number
- Include at least one special character

### Password Strength Indicator

The password strength meter shows:
- **Weak** (Red) - Easily guessable
- **Fair** (Orange) - Could be stronger
- **Good** (Yellow) - Acceptable strength
- **Strong** (Green) - Recommended

## Account Security

### Two-Factor Authentication (if available)

1. Go to **Settings > Security**
2. Enable **Two-Factor Authentication**
3. Follow the setup instructions
4. Save your backup codes

### Active Sessions

View and manage your active login sessions:

1. Go to **Settings > Security > Active Sessions**
2. Review connected devices
3. Click **Sign Out** on any session you don't recognize

## Privacy Settings

### Data Visibility

Control what information is visible:

- **Public Profile** - Visible to all users
- **Limited** - Visible only to your team
- **Private** - Visible only to you

### Communication Preferences

Manage how we contact you:

- Email notifications
- Push notifications
- SMS alerts (if available)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Update Email

Your email may be linked to your organization's directory. Contact your administrator to request changes.

### Profile Picture Not Saving

- Check file size (max 5MB)
- Try a different image format
- Clear your browser cache and try again

### Password Change Failed

- Ensure current password is correct
- New password must meet all requirements
- Try a different, unique password
