# Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about IDICO IPMS.

## General

### What is IDICO IPMS?

IDICO IPMS (Industrial Park Management System) is a platform for managing industrial park operations, providing tools for communication, reporting, and administration.

### What platforms does IDICO IPMS support?

IDICO IPMS is available on:
- **Web browsers** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- **Android** devices (Google Play Store)
- **iOS** devices (App Store)
- **PWA** (installable web app)

### Is IDICO IPMS free?

IDICO IPMS is provided by your organization. Contact your administrator for access and licensing information.

### What languages are supported?

Currently, IDICO IPMS supports:
- English
- Vietnamese (Tiếng Việt)

More languages may be added in future updates.

## Account

### How do I create an account?

Accounts are created by your organization's administrator. Contact them to request access.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Create a new password

### Can I change my email address?

Your email may be linked to your organization's directory. Contact your administrator to request an email change.

### How do I delete my account?

Contact your organization's administrator to request account deletion. Note that this action is permanent.

## Features

### How do I enable dark mode?

Go to **Settings > Appearance** and select "Dark" theme, or use the theme toggle in the header.

### Can I use IDICO IPMS offline?

Yes! As a Progressive Web App (PWA), IDICO IPMS can cache content for offline access. Previously visited pages will be available without internet.

### How do I receive push notifications?

1. Allow notifications when prompted by your browser/device
2. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
3. Enable the notification categories you want

### Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to **Settings > Privacy > Data** and click "Export My Data" to download your information.

## Mobile

### Is there a mobile app?

Yes! IDICO IPMS is available on:
- Google Play Store (Android)
- App Store (iOS)

You can also install the PWA from your browser.

### How do I install the PWA?

**Chrome Desktop**: Click the install icon in the address bar

**Mobile Chrome**: Tap the menu and select "Add to Home Screen"

**Safari iOS**: Tap Share, then "Add to Home Screen"

### Do I need to download updates?

- **App Store/Play Store**: Updates are automatic or available through the store
- **Web/PWA**: Updates are automatic when you reload the app

## Security

### How is my data protected?

IDICO IPMS uses:
- HTTPS encryption for all connections
- Secure authentication with JWT tokens
- Regular security updates
- Industry-standard data protection practices

### What should I do if I notice suspicious activity?

1. Change your password immediately
2. Review your login history in Settings
3. Sign out of all devices
4. Contact your administrator

### Is two-factor authentication available?

Two-factor authentication may be available depending on your organization's configuration. Check **Settings > Security** or contact your administrator.

## Troubleshooting

### The app is running slowly. What can I do?

1. Clear your browser cache
2. Close unused tabs
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try a different browser
5. See the [Troubleshooting Guide](/docs/user/guides/troubleshooting) for more solutions

### I'm not receiving notifications.

1. Check browser/device notification settings
2. Verify notifications are enabled in app Settings
3. Check if Do Not Disturb is active
4. See [Notification Guide](/docs/user/features/notifications) for detailed steps

### Features are not working properly.

1. Refresh the page
2. Clear cache and cookies
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Try incognito/private mode
5. Contact support if the issue persists

## Contact & Support

### How do I get help?

- Check this FAQ and documentation
- Use the in-app support feature
- Contact your organization's administrator
- Email support (if available for your organization)

### How do I report a bug?

Go to **Settings > Support > Report Issue** and describe the problem with as much detail as possible.

### How do I suggest a feature?

Contact your administrator or use the feedback option in the app to suggest new features.
